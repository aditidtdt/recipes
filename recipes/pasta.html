Homemade Pasta

Description

Why buy the boxed stuff when you can make noodles at home? This homemade pasta recipe is way better than what you can buy at the store — and it’s surprisingly easy to throw together with just five ingredients you likely already have on hand!

This homemade pasta recipe is consistently great and easy to make with flour, eggs, salt, olive oil, and water. Roll out by hand or use a pasta machine to cut dough into desired pasta shape.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 tablespoons water

Directions

  1. Combine flour and salt in a medium bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center and add olive oil and beaten eggs. Mix well until a stiff dough forms, adding up to 2 tablespoons water if needed.
  3. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth, 3 to 4 minutes. Wrap dough and let rest for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  4. Roll dough by hand or with a pasta machine to desired thickness, then cut into strips of desired width and length.
