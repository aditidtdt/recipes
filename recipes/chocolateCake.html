CHOCOLATE CAKE

Description

No need to reach for a box cake mix anymore when there's a super-easy, no-fuss, no-baking-skills-required, rich, and chocolatey chocolate cake that anyone can bake from scratch.

You've officially run out of reasons to say you can't bake a cake because this is the easiest recipe for making a chocolate cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • ½ cup vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two nine-inch round pans. OR line a half sheet pan with parchment paper and spray with cooking spray if you want to bake a sheet cake.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer.
  4. Stir in the boiling water last. The batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  5. Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven until you can insert a toothpick into the cake, and remove it dry. OR if you're baking a sheet cake, bake for only 15 minutes. Cool in the pan(s) for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
